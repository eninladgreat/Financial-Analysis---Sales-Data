This document provides a comprehensive analysis of a financial dataset spanning September 2013 to December 2014, covering five countries (USA, Canada, France, Germany, Mexico) and six products (Paseo, VTT, Velo, Amarilla, Montana, Carretera).  The analysis was conducted using Microsoft Power BI Desktop and includes detailed documentation of the data model, DAX measures, and visualizations. 

Key Highlights:

1.	Business Performance:

o	Total Gross Sales: $127.93M (248% growth from 2013 to 2014). 
o	Net Income: $10.50M (8.21% Gross Margin). 
o	High-volume, low-margin business model with 91.79% of revenue consumed by costs. 

2.	Country Performance:

o	USA leads in gross sales ($25.03M) but ranks 4th in profit ($3.00M). 
o	France is the most profitable market, converting revenue to profit efficiently. 
o	Mexico is the weakest market in both revenue ($20.95M) and profit ($2.91M), presenting growth opportunities. 

3.	Product Performance:

o	Paseo is the top-performing product ($33.01M total sales, 27.8% of revenue) with exceptional growth (+2,052% in December 2014). 
o	VTT experienced an 81% decline in December 2014, requiring urgent investigation. 
o	Carretera showed significant growth (+342% in December 2014), signaling emerging demand. 


4.	Cost Structure:

o	COGS accounts for 79.6% of gross sales, significantly above industry benchmarks (60–70%). 
o	Discounts eroded $9.21M (7.2% of gross sales), requiring policy reform. 
o	Other expenses totaled $6.40M (5% of gross sales). 

5.	Strategic Priorities:

o	Reduce COGS by 3% to save ~$3.84M annually. 
o	Implement discount governance to reduce average discount rate from 7% to 5%, saving $2.56M annually. 
o	Expand Mexico market to achieve 20% revenue growth. 
o	Investigate VTT's decline and reposition if necessary. 
o	Improve gross margin from 8.21% to 15% over three years. 

Tools & Methodology:

•	Power BI Desktop: Used for dashboard design, data modeling, and DAX measure engineering. 
•	Data Model: 7 tables (1 fact table, 1 calendar table, 1 unpivoted analytical table, 4 dummy/reference tables). 
•	Visualizations: Waterfall charts, bar charts, pie charts, line trends, and KPI cards. 

Dashboard Pages:

1.	Country Performance: Benchmarks revenue and profit across five countries. 
2.	Income Statement: Waterfall chart showing cost deductions from gross sales to net income. 
3.	Quarterly & Trend Analysis: Quarterly P&L breakdown and 16-month sales trend. 
4.	Product Performance: Product-level revenue analysis, Canada-specific sales, and December YoY comparison. 
5.	Cost & Margin Analysis: Detailed cost structure and margin analytics. 

Strategic Insights:

The analysis highlights critical areas for improvement, including reducing COGS, reforming discount policies, and addressing product and market-specific challenges.  The document serves as a professional portfolio project showcasing advanced Power BI and DAX capabilities for financial analysis.
